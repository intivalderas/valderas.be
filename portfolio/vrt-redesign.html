UX Design Public Media Research

VRT Digital Experience

Designing user-centered digital experiences for Flanders' public broadcaster, reaching millions of viewers across web, mobile, and connected TV platforms.

[ Project hero image ]

The Challenge

As a public broadcaster, VRT serves a diverse audience with varying needs and digital literacy levels. The challenge was to create cohesive, accessible experiences that make content discovery effortless while maintaining the editorial integrity that viewers trust.

My Role

As UX Designer, I led user research, interaction design, and usability testing across multiple product teams. My work focused on understanding audience behaviour through data and research, then translating those insights into intuitive interfaces.

[ Process / wireframes ]

Approach

Starting from the user's perspective — always. Every design decision was grounded in research, whether through user interviews, analytics review, or prototype testing with real audiences. I worked closely with developers, product managers, and editorial teams to ensure designs were both feasible and aligned with content strategy.
