About

A bit about me

I'm Inti Valderas Caro, a UX & Interaction Designer based in Sint-Niklaas, Belgium. I hold a Master's degree in Interaction Design and currently design digital experiences at VRT, Flanders' public broadcaster.

Beyond my work at VRT, I serve as a board member at Open Knowledge Belgium and coordinate the Open Summer of Code — connecting students with open-source civic tech projects.

Through Valderas, I consult on interactive strategy and design, having worked with organisations like KBC, Engie, and LUCA School of Arts.

I'm particularly fascinated by the intersection of interaction design, machine learning, and artificial intelligence — always looking for ways to make complex systems feel simple and human.
