About

A bit about me

I'm Inti Valderas Caro, a UX & Interaction Designer based in Sint-Niklaas, Belgium. I hold a Master's degree in Interaction Design and currently design digital experiences at VRT MAX, Flanders' public broadcaster, as a UX Design consultant via iO.

Beyond my work at VRT MAX, I serve as a board member at Open Knowledge Belgium and coordinate the Open Summer of Code — connecting students with open-source civic tech projects.

Through Valderas, I consult on interactive strategy, design, and operational tooling — from setting up Zoho One for Nitra to marketing automations and CRM data workflows. I've also worked with organisations like KBC, Smooth Sailing, Engie, and LUCA School of Arts.

I'm particularly fascinated by the intersection of interaction design, machine learning, and artificial intelligence — a topic I've also explored in a talk at UX Beers — always looking for ways to make complex systems feel simple and human.
